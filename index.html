Our Story

Crafted with Love in the Philippines
MiniMe Boutique

More than clothing.
It's a memory.

MiniMe was born from a mother's desire to dress her family in something beautiful that lasted longer than a single photo. Cheska and Gab started this boutique in the Philippines with a single collection and a belief that matching outfits should never compromise quality or comfort.

Every set is made with natural fibers — breathable, gentle on skin, and designed to be worn for celebrations, weekends, and ordinary Tuesday mornings that turn into precious memories.

Made with Love

Every stitch is done with care and intention for your family.

Heirloom Quality

Pieces designed to be kept, worn again, and passed on.

Philippine Made

Supporting local artisans and craftsmanship from our home.

Natural Fibers

Breathable, soft, and gentle for babies and toddlers.
